Cosmic Wonders

\n\n
\n

A day on Venus is longer than its year. It takes Venus 243 Earth days to rotate once on its axis, but only 225 Earth days to orbit the Sun.

Did You Know?

\n

Venus rotates in the opposite direction to most other planets, meaning the Sun rises in the west and sets in the east.
