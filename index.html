Deep Sea Wonders

\n\n
\n

The Mariana Trench is the deepest oceanic trench on Earth, reaching a maximum known depth of about 11,000 meters (36,000 feet). If Mount Everest were placed in the trench, its peak would still be over a mile underwater.

\n \n
\n\n
\n

Did You Know?

\n

Despite the extreme pressure and lack of sunlight, the Mariana Trench is home to a diverse array of unique organisms, including giant amphipods and snailfish, adapted to survive in these harsh conditions.
