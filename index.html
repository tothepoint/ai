Exoplanet 55 Cancri e: A Diamond World

Scientists have identified exoplanet candidates, like 55 Cancri e, that may have surfaces made of graphite and diamond.

This 'super-Earth' is twice the size of Earth and eight times more massive, potentially hiding a diamond core comparable in size to the Moon and 100,000 times heavier than Earth!
