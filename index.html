Oceanic Wonders

The deepest part of the ocean is the Mariana Trench, which is about 11,000 meters (36,000 feet) deep. If Mount Everest were placed in the Mariana Trench, its peak would still be over a mile underwater.

Did You Know?

The pressure at the bottom of the Mariana Trench is over 1,000 times the standard atmospheric pressure at sea level.
