The Pillars of Creation: Stellar Nurseries

The Pillars of Creation are towering cosmic structures of interstellar gas and dust, where new stars are born.

Located in the Eagle Nebula, these majestic formations are sculpted by the intense ultraviolet light from massive, young stars, slowly eroding away while simultaneously fostering new stellar life within their dense clouds.
