Did You Know?

\n

The Mariana Trench is located in the western Pacific Ocean and is the deepest oceanic trench on Earth. Its deepest point, Challenger Deep, is approximately 11,000 meters (36,000 feet) deep. To put that into perspective, if Mount Everest were placed in the trench, its peak would still be over a mile below the surface!
