Interstellar Odyssey

Voyager's Grand Tour

Voyager 1, launched in 1977, crossed into interstellar space in 2012, becoming the first human-made probe to do so.

Voyager 1 continues to operate, sending back data from beyond our solar system. It carries a Golden Record, a phonograph record containing sounds and images selected to portray the diversity of life and culture on Earth, intended for any intelligent extraterrestrial life form who may find it.
