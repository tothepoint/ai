Ocean Depths

The Mariana Trench is the deepest oceanic trench on Earth, reaching a maximum known depth of about 11,034 meters (36,201 feet).

Did You Know?

If Mount Everest were placed in the Mariana Trench, its peak would still be over a mile underwater.
