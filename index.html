WASP-12b: The Egg-Shaped Exoplanet

\n

WASP-12b is an exoplanet so close to its star that it\'s being ripped apart, resembling a \'hot Jupiter\' with an egg-like shape.

\n

This gas giant orbits its star in just over a day, experiencing extreme tidal forces that distort its shape and cause its atmosphere to be stripped away. It\'s one of the darkest known exoplanets, absorbing almost 94% of the light that hits it.
